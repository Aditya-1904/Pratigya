PUBLIC SPEAKING

Public speaking event instills confidence and courage in the minds of young Pratigyans as they speak about various topics through their hearts. The students are encouraged to come out of their comfort zone, and try their speaking skills. Helping inculcate the confidence to speak in front of the masses.
