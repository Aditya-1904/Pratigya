Igniting Minds & Warming Hearts

PRATIGYA–“PLEDGE” to build a community free of illiteracy, full of knowledge, experiences and self-development in and around TIET campus, Patiala.

PRATIGYA is an effort to shape the dreams which every kid nurtures in his or her thoughts. We just give our best to help them find a way to achieve what they desired.

PRATIGYA’s root is its team which basically teaches the underprivileged kids from classes 1 to 10+2 as well as diploma classes. These kids include those of the children of fourth class employees of Thapar university as well as those residing in Patiala. Evolved in 2005, PRATIGYA, an NGO, is one of the biggest and most active societies in TIET, Patiala.

TIET generously supports our initiative with infrastructural, financial, administrative and advisory help. Currently, Pratigya Abhiyan is guided and directed by
Dr. Anoop Verma
(Associate Professor, School of Energy & Environment)
